Milkha Singh - The Flying Sikh

Milkha Singh, also known as The Flying Sikh, was one of the greatest athletes in Indian history. His story is one of triumph, perseverance, and dedication.

Major Achievements

  • 1958 Asian Games Gold Medals (200m and 400m)
  • 1958 Commonwealth Games Gold Medal (440 yards)
  • 1962 Asian Games Gold Medals (400m and 4x400m relay)
  • Set several national and international records

Legacy

Milkha Singh's life is an inspiration to millions. His journey from adversity to becoming a global icon is a testament to his indomitable spirit.
